About

Hello! I am Kayla Dennis and I am getting a BA in Web Design and Development from University of Arkansas at Little Rock. I am also getting a minor in Graphic Design and love to design websites and artwork. I strive to gain more expirence in my degree field and create for the web.

  • Volunteer Work

    I have done volunteer work for the Lantern Festival (2019), and volunteered multiple times at the UA Little Rock campus garden(2019, 2020). Voltuneering has helped me become a more well rounded person.

  • Out in the Field

    I have done some freelance graphic design work for a collegue and made a website for a non-profit organization. Completeing the IT Minor through UA Little Rock has expanded my horizons and helped me gain expirence with working in different IDE's and CMS's.

  • Design Work

    I am working towards a minor in graphic design and an proficent in most creative cloud software, ie: Photoshop, Illustrator, and Indesign. I am profiecent in Microsoft Office, ie: Word, Powerpoint, and Excel. I have used to skills to design wedding invitations, manuals, and other documents.

  • Social Skills

    I have completed courses in speech and soft skills, ie: presentations, teamwork, and communication. I have also worked as a stuent outreach respresenative for UA Little Rock, which has improved my ability to relay imformation and interact with different types of people.
